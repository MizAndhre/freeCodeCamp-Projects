pCloud is your personal cloud space where you can store all your files and folders.

It has a user-friendly interface that clearly shows where everything is located and what it does.

About

pCloud is a secure and easy-to-use service that lets you store up to 10GB for free, offers lifetime plans for up to 2TB and generally will make storing files easier than most of the competition.

The software is available for almost any devices and platforms – iOS and Android devices, MacOSX, Windows OS, and all Linux distributions.

Features

Access on all your devices

Save your files and keep them on your laptop with pCloud Drive, on your phone or on the web

Share and collaborate

Send, relive and work with friends on every file

Unbreakable security

Keep your private files confidential with pCloud Crypto
